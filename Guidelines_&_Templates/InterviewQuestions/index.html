    CivicTechWR Community Outreach Interview Questions¶

    About the Organization¶

    1. Mission: What is the primary mission or goal of your organization?
    2. Community Impact: What impact are you hoping to have on the community?
    3. Target Audience: Who do you primarily serve, and what are their biggest challenges?

    Challenges and Needs¶

    1. Current Challenges: What are the biggest challenges your organization is currently facing?
    2. Technology: How does your organization currently use technology to achieve its goals? Are there any gaps or frustrations?
    3. Collaboration: What kind of partnerships or collaborations would help your organization thrive?

    Opportunities for Support¶

    1. CivicTech Potential: Are there ways CivicTechWR could support your work through technology, tools, or training?
    2. Resources: What resources (technical, financial, or human) do you need most right now?
    3. Volunteer Opportunities: Are there any ways volunteers with technical skills could help your organization?

    Community Engagement¶

    1. Engagement Strategies: How does your organization currently engage with the community?
    2. Feedback: How do you gather feedback from the people you serve? Are there ways we could assist in improving that process?

    Vision for the Future¶

    1. Growth: Where do you see your organization in five years? What are the key steps to get there?
    2. Innovation: Are there any innovative ideas you’d like to explore but haven’t had the resources to implement?

    Closing¶

    1. Unique Insights: Is there something unique about your organization or community that we should know?
    2. Open Suggestions: If you could design any kind of tool, event, or program to help your organization or community, what would it look like?

    We're a community group that brings together people from different sectors and industries to actively solve issues facing our local community using design and technology

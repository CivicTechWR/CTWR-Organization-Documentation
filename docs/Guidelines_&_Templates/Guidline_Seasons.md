## Seasons Format

- Week 1: Pitching & Ideation 💡
- Week 2: Mini hackathon - prottyping - grading solutions
- Week 3: Buffer week, if you need to spend one more week on another mini hackathon 
- Week 4 to 11: Work on your project 🛠️
- Week 12: DEMO DAY!! 🎉
